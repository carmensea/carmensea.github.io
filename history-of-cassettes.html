Remember these?

This is a cassette. What goes on it can be totally magical.

Back in the day, you had to sit by the radio and wait for your song to come on. Once you heard the first note, you'd quickly press record.

Imagine that you had a list of songs that you thought were perfect for the one you loved. Or maybe you were into the heavy, emotional music that you really wanted to share with someone. You'd have to listen all day to the radio for the right song, in the right order.

Lucky for you, today we have "Spotify". Gone are the days of waiting to hear a certain song. Gone are the days of rewinding a tape. Now, you can drag and drop.
