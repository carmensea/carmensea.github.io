What makes a good playlist?

A good playlist is more than just a bunch of good songs thrown in random order.

A great, memorable playlist is more personal than it is random.

You're asking to take up minutes-to hours of someone's day.

This means you should put ample time in creating something for them.

Some things to consider:

  • For whom is the playlist?
  • What message are you trying to convey?
  • Is there a certain song you feel encompasses your message already?

Sometimes a certain song will inspire you to make an entire playlist. This is helpful, because you'll already know your message. If not, narrowing down your audience, and figuring out your message will be immensely helpful.
